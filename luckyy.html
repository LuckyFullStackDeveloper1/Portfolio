Hardik Pandya
Born on 11 October 1993 in Surat, Gujarat. Moved to Vadodara for cricket training. Enrolled in Kiran More’s academy. His father supported his cricket career by shutting his business and relocating.

Shree Ram
Avatar of Lord Surya, born as the son of King Dashrath. His life and values are detailed in the Valmiki Ramayana. A symbol of dharma and sacrifice, paralleled with Karna in Mahabharata.

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel
(1875–1950) Indian independence leader and first Deputy PM of India. Played a major role in political integration post-independence. Known as the Iron Man of India.
